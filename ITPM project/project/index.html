COMPUTING

The BSC Honours Degree programme of SLIIT in Information Technology is of 4 years' duration with two semesters per year. During a semester four to five courses which carry a total of 16 - 20 credits are conducted. A course carries 3 credits when it involves 3 student contact hours each week or 4 credits when it involves 4-5 student contact hours each week for a semester.
